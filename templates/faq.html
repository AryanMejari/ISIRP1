Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our publication process

Payment & Pricing

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and PayPal. All payments are securely processed through Razorpay.
The ₹200 publication fee covers our review system, plagiarism check, digital certification, and platform maintenance. This nominal fee helps us keep the service accessible while maintaining high standards.
Yes, all payments are processed through Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We do not store any payment information on our servers.

Paper Submission

Our review system typically completes the initial assessment within 15 minutes. Certificates are generated and emailed within 15 minutes after successful evaluation.
We accept DOCX and PDF formats for manuscripts (max 10MB). Supplementary materials can be uploaded as ZIP, PDF, or DOCX (max 20MB).

Certificates & Publication

Digital certificates are generated automatically within 15 minutes after successful evaluation of the paper and emailed to the corresponding author. You can also download them from your dashboard.
