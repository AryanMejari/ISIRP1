AI-Assisted Review Process

Enhancing research evaluation with artificial intelligence

At ISIRP Research Journal, we use Artificial Intelligence (AI) to assist in the initial review of submitted research papers. This process is designed to ensure a faster, unbiased, and consistent evaluation of basic submission standards.

Our AI review system helps maintain high standards while reducing the time authors wait for initial feedback.

What the AI Review Covers

Structure & Formatting

Checks that the paper follows a coherent structure (title, abstract, introduction, methodology, results, references).

Language & Clarity

Identifies grammar, spelling, and readability issues.

Plagiarism Screening

Compares content against online sources to detect potential duplication.

Reference Check

Ensures citations are present and properly formatted.

What the AI Review Does Not Do

Scientific Evaluation

Evaluate the scientific accuracy or technical validity of results.

Replace Peer Review

Replace traditional peer review by subject matter experts.

Decision Making

Guarantee acceptance or rejection based on academic merit.

Why We Use AI Review

Speed

Papers can be screened within minutes instead of weeks.

Accessibility

Authors receive instant feedback for improvements.

Fairness

Removes human bias in initial screening.

Note: The AI review is an assistive tool. Final responsibility for the quality, accuracy, and originality of the content remains with the author(s).
