10. Use of Metrics

Any metrics or scores displayed (such as Research Visibility Index - RVI) are calculated internally and are not affiliated with Clarivate Analytics, Scopus, or any other external indexing services. We use "Citation Score" to represent the research paper's impact.

Research Visibility Index (RVI) Formula:

RVI = (Total Views + Total Downloads) / Total Papers Published
  • Total Views: Number of times all papers have been viewed
  • Total Downloads: Number of times all papers have been downloaded
  • Total Papers Published: Number of papers in the repository

This metric is provided for informational purposes only and should not be considered an official measure of research impact.
