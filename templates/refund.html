Refund Policy

Last updated: July 31, 2025

1. General Policy

The publication fee of ₹200 is generally non-refundable once the paper submission process has begun. This is because our review system begins processing immediately upon submission.

2. Eligibility for Refund

Refunds may be considered in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Technical failure on our part that prevents processing of your submission
  • Duplicate payment for the same paper
  • Payment made by mistake before submission was completed

3. Non-Refundable Situations

No refunds will be provided for:

  • Papers rejected after review for quality or plagiarism issues
  • Author changing their mind after submission
  • Incorrect information provided during submission
  • Delays in certificate generation due to technical issues

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, email teamisirp@gmail.com within 7 days of payment with:
- Your Paper ID
- Payment transaction ID
- Reason for refund request

Refunds will be processed within 14 business days to the original payment method.

5. Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us may result in permanent suspension of your account and rejection of all future submissions.

For any questions regarding our refund policy, please contact us at teamisirp@gmail.com
